### Quote
- Definition：A non-binding price offer created from a customer's selected products; on this site a "quote" is submitted either as a mailto email of the cart contents or as a Shopify Draft Order tagged `Website quote request` / `Awaiting sales review` for the sales desk to follow up.
- Aliases：quote request、build a quote

### Trade account
- Definition：A B2B customer profile used by Spares Automation; trade-account holders use the quote workflow instead of direct online checkout, and may apply through the `/trade-account` route.
- Aliases：trade account

### Part finder form
- Definition：The homepage form where customers submit a part number or description; submissions are routed via email (mailto) to the sales team rather than through a database.
- Aliases：part finder、product questions form

### Asphalt / Blacktop spares
- Definition：One of the four main product lines sold by Spares Automation, covering feeders, burner/drying parts, bitumen pumps, baghouse filters and mixing tower liners for asphalt plants.
- Aliases：asphalt spares、blacktop spares

### Ready-Mix / Concrete spares
- Definition：Product line covering aggregate feeding, cement silos, additive systems, water/air controls and automation components for ready-mix concrete plants.
- Aliases：concrete spares、ready-mix spares

### Packing machinery spares
- Definition：Product line for sealers, rollers, belts and other packing-line replacement parts.
- Aliases：packing spares

### Automation & drives
- Definition：Product line covering VFDs, PLC modules, relays and sensors for industrial automation.
- Aliases：automation spares、drives

### Home controls
- Definition：Product line for smart relays, sensors and DIN rail supplies aimed at home/building automation.
- Aliases：home automation

### Control panels & software
- Definition：Product line for custom PLC panels and associated software/support; replaced the legacy "New Arrivals" entry on the homepage.
- Aliases：control panels
